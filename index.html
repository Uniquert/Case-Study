    I have tested 10 different images.

    GOOGLE LENS has predicted 10/10 of the images correctly.

    Meanwhile, MobileNet only predicted 2/10 images correctly, doing much worse compared to GOOGLE LENS.

    In conclusion, GOOGLE LENS is able to cleary and precisely predict and scan images to get the most precise result.

    On the Other hand, MobileNet struggles to get a prediction, takes much more time to predict, and is a lot less accurate or precise.
